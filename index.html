Who This Is For

This intake process is for:

  • Architects exploring exterior or interior wood applications
  • Designers coordinating finishes across multiple scopes
  • Builders validating feasibility before pricing
  • Owners or developers seeking early clarity

Typical project types

  • Custom residential
  • Mountain modern homes
  • Hospitality and commercial feature applications
Why This Exists

Custom wood packages create problems when introduced too late:

  • Profiles and applications lock prematurely
  • Finish intent doesn't translate across exterior and interior uses
  • Lead times surface after approvals
  • Teams discover misalignment after design decisions are final

This process introduces the right mill earlier — without sales pressure.

What Submitting Plans Does

Submitting plans allows us to:

  • Understand basic project scope and application intent
  • Confirm whether the project is appropriate for mill review
  • Route qualified projects directly to the mill for evaluation

Important: Springer Millworks does not provide architectural, technical, or pricing review. Plans are collected to coordinate mill consideration and introduction.

About the Mill

Projects that align with scope and application are introduced to Origin Millworks, a U.S.-based mill specializing in custom reclaimed and pre-finished wood packages for residential and commercial projects.

All pricing, quoting, and production are handled by the mill.
